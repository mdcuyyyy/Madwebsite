My Experiences

My journey so far has been full of experiences that challenged me, taught me important lessons, and helped me grow in many ways. Here are some of the highlights of what I have done :

Hiking

This is a hiking trip I did with my housemates at Bukit Keluang. We were very excited and started our journey at 4 a.m. After performing the Subuh prayer, we began our climb to reach the peak in time to watch the sunrise. We arrived safely and were greeted with a calm and beautiful view from the top.

Adventure Activities

This is my rock climbing experience at Gua Musang in Muse EcoPark. It pushed me out of my comfort zone because I had to climb a hill and face my fear of heights. I kept going until I reached the topand finally i tried the flying fox. This was a very valuable experience and i truly enjoyed every moment of it.

Part-Time Job

During the semester 3 break, I started working part-time at a Thai restaurant called Padi Restaurant. This gave me a valuable experience as i learned the true meaning of earning money through hard work. Even during the fasting month, I managed to get through the work and adapt despite facing a language barrier with the other staff who were from Thailand.

Internship

As part of our Semester 5 course for the Diploma in Information Management, we were required to complete a two-month internship during the Semester 4 break. During this internship, we took on responsibilities similar to regular staff. I completed my practical training at the National Archives of Malaysia, Kelantan branch. This experience taught me many lessons and gave me understanding that I had only learned from notes before, allowing me to apply knowledge in a real, practical environment and learn more deeply.
